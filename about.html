The Awareness Gap

Bridging the
Silence.

Research shows that 96% of individuals remain unaware of available support systems. Finding verified help in a crisis should not be another obstacle. Sakhi 181 centralizes verified data to ensure no one has to walk alone.

What we provide

A free, secure platform designed to discover One Stop Centers nearby. We provide comprehensive details, verified contact information, and photos to ensure you know exactly where you're going.

How we do it

Through cutting edge location intelligence and a rigorous verification process, we keep our database continuously updated. We bridge the gap between government resources and those who need them most.

Why it matters

Life-saving speed is critical in moments of crisis. By bringing clarity, dignity, and hope, we help individuals navigate their darkest hours with the confidence of verified support.

Our Mission

"We are committed to creating a reliable, accessible, and informed support ecosystem for individuals seeking help from GBV. Our goal is to ensure that no one feels lost or alone in moments of crisis."

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make and every partnership we forge.

Trust

We earn and uphold the trust of individuals by safeguarding their privacy and offering verified, dependable resources.

Efficiency

We ensure timely access to help through streamlined systems and accurate, up-to-date information.

Collaboration

We work hand-in-hand with NGOs, communities, and government bodies to strengthen the support network across the country.
